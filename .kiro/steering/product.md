# Product Overview

This is a **Tunisian accounting management system** built for accountants (comptables) and their clients. The application handles:

- **Journal d'Achat** (Purchase Journal): Supplier invoices with Tunisian tax rates (TVA 7%, 13%, 19%)
- **Journal de Vente** (Sales Journal): Customer invoices with tax calculations
- **Journal Banque** (Bank Journal): Bank statement movements and reconciliation
- **Plan Comptable** (Chart of Accounts): Customizable accounting plans per accountant

## Key Features

- AI-powered invoice scanning and data extraction using Google Gemini
- Automated accounting entry generation (both AI and rule-based)
- Multi-client management for accountants
- Role-based access control (admin, comptable, client)
- Excel export functionality
- Cloudinary integration for invoice image storage

## User Roles

- **Admin**: System administration
- **Comptable**: Accountants managing multiple clients and their accounting
- **Client**: Business owners viewing their own financial data

## Tunisian Accounting Context

The system follows Tunisian accounting standards with specific tax rates, timbre fiscal (stamp duty), and chart of accounts numbering conventions.
